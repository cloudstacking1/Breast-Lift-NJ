What is Plastic Surgeon?

A plastic surgeon is a specialized medical professional trained in cosmetic and reconstructive surgery to enhance appearance or restore function after injury, illness, or congenital conditions. They perform procedures like breast augmentation, liposuction, rhinoplasty, facelifts, Botox, and reconstructive surgeries to help patients achieve their aesthetic or medical goals.
